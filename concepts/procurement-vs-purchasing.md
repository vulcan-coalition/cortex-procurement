# Procurement vs Purchasing

These terms are often used interchangeably but they are fundamentally different.

## Comparison

| Aspect | Procurement (Strategic) | Purchasing (Operational) |
|--------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| Level | Strategic — long-term planning | Operational — daily transactions |
| Activities | Sourcing & supplier management, contract negotiation, vendor evaluation | Create PO, track delivery, process invoices |
| Focus | Value, risk, relationships | Execution, speed, accuracy |

## Why This Matters

The distinction affects how the system is designed:

- **Procurement** involves decision-making, evaluation, and strategy — this is where analytical tools and scoring engines add value
- **Purchasing** involves transaction processing — this is where workflow automation and integration with ERP matter most
