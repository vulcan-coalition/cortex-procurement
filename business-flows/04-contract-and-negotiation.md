# Step 4: Contract & Negotiation

## Overview

After a vendor is selected, the procurement team negotiates detailed terms and formalizes the agreement in a **Contract or Framework Agreement**. This step covers pricing, duration, delivery conditions, penalties, and SLAs.

## Actors

- **Procurement Team** — leads negotiation and contract drafting
- **Legal** — reviews contract terms and compliance
- **Selected Vendor** — negotiates and signs the agreement
- **Finance** — validates payment terms and budget alignment

## Process Steps

```
Negotiate Terms → Draft Contract → Legal Review → Sign Contract → Store & Track
```

1. **Negotiate Terms** — discuss pricing, delivery schedule, SLAs, penalties, and warranty
2. **Draft Contract** — procurement prepares the contract document
3. **Legal Review** — legal team reviews for compliance and risk
4. **Sign Contract** — both parties sign the formal agreement
5. **Store & Track** — contract is stored with renewal alerts and key dates tracked

## Key Contract Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|------------|
| Parties | Buyer and vendor details |
| Scope | Goods/services covered |
| Pricing | Agreed unit prices, total value, currency |
| Duration | Contract period with start and end dates |
| Delivery Terms | Schedule, location, acceptance criteria |
| Payment Terms | e.g., Net 30 days after GRN |
| Penalties | Late delivery penalties (e.g., 0.1% per day) |
| SLA | Service level commitments and response times |
| Warranty | Coverage period and terms |
| Termination | Conditions under which either party can terminate |
