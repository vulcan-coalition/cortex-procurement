# Standard Delivery Service Level Agreement

**Vendor:** NextIT Co., Ltd.
**Customer:** [To be filled upon contract]
**Effective Date:** Upon PO confirmation
**Version:** 2.1 (Updated January 2026)

---

## 1. Delivery Timeline

| Order Value (THB) | Bangkok Metro | Provincial | International |
|-------------------|--------------|-----------|---------------|
| < 100,000 | 5 business days | 7 business days | 14 business days |
| 100,000 – 500,000 | 3 business days | 5 business days | 10 business days |
| > 500,000 | **2 business days** | 3 business days | 7 business days |

## 2. Delivery Conditions

- All deliveries include unpacking and visual inspection at delivery point
- Delivery personnel will obtain recipient signature on delivery note
- Delivery to specific floor/room available for Bangkok Metro at no extra charge
- Provincial deliveries include doorstep delivery only; floor delivery +1,000 THB

## 3. Penalty for Late Delivery

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| 1-3 business days | Warning letter |
| 4-7 business days | 0.5% of order value per day |
| 8-14 business days | 1.0% of order value per day |
| > 14 business days | Buyer may cancel order; full refund within 7 days |

Maximum penalty cap: 10% of total order value

## 4. Handling Damaged / Defective Goods

- Report within 3 business days of delivery
- Vendor to provide replacement within 5 business days
- If replacement not available, full credit note issued within 10 business days
- Vendor bears all return shipping costs

## 5. Communication SLA

| Event | Response Time |
|-------|-------------|
| Delivery status inquiry | Within 4 hours (business hours) |
| Damage report | Within 2 hours (business hours) |
| Replacement request | Confirmation within 1 business day |
| Invoice dispute | Resolution within 5 business days |
